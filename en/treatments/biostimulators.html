Frequently Asked Questions about Biostimulators

CaHA vs. PLLA - which type suits me?

CaHA (Radiesse): Immediate lifting effect + collagen building. PLLA (Sculptra): Pure biostimulation over 6 months. Decision based on skin type.

When are first results visible?

CaHA: Immediately visible + further improvement over 12 months. PLLA: After 6-8 weeks through collagen synthesis.

Can biostimulators be combined?

Yes, optimally combinable with microneedling, PRP or hyaluronic acid for synergistic effects and accelerated regeneration.

How do they differ from fillers?

Biostimulators activate your body's own regeneration instead of volume replacement. More natural, longer-lasting results.

How long do results last?

18-24 months through sustainable building of your body's own structures. Gradual degradation, more natural than fillers.

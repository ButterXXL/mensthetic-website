Frequently Asked Questions

Is the treatment painful?

Minimally. With numbing cream and ultra-fine needles, treatment is very comfortable.

When will I see results?

Initial freshness immediately, optimal results after 2-3 sessions as collagen builds up.

How long do results last?

6-12 months depending on lifestyle. Regular maintenance extends results.

Can I work immediately after?

Yes, you're immediately presentable. Minor redness fades within hours.

Difference to fillers?

Meso-Eyes regenerates and revitalizes, fillers add volume. Often combined for best results.

Suitable for all skin types?

Yes, individually adapted to skin type and specific male anatomy.
